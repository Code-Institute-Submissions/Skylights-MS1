Meet The Band

Skylights are an indie rock band hailing from York and Leeds. The band members consist of Vocalist – Rob Scarisbrick, Guitarist – Turnbull Smith, Drummer - Myles Soley and Bassist – Jonny Scarisbrick. The band are heavily influenced by the sounds of Leeds band – The Music, Manchester’s Oasis, Wigan’s The Verve as well as the punkier tones of the likes of Sex Pistols and The Clash.

Many people are backing Skylights to reach the very pinnacle of their profession. They have amassed a huge following, especially in Yorkshire. Famous faces such as professional boxer, Josh Warrington and Leeds United manager Marcelo Bielsa have shown their support for the band.

Discography

Skylights debut physical single Enemies hit number two in the UK Physical Singles Chart in June, held back from the top spot only by Fontaines DC.

Skylights released their latest single, Darkness Falls, earlier this year. The release comes from 42’s Records and is available on limited edition vinyl. Darkness Falls sets a different tone to the previous releases and shows the band’s ability to provide a slower pace track without losing any of the energy. It has an instant air of hope and calm amongst the fear and unknowingness that surrounds us in these uncertain times.
